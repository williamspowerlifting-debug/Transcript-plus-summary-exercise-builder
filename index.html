📖 How to Use

Create a listening exercise in under 2 minutes.

0. Choose exercise type

Transcript Gap Fill — students fill gaps in the spoken transcript sentence by sentence with video playback.

Summary Gap Fill — an AI summary of the video is generated; students fill all gaps at once while the video plays freely.

1. Upload a video

Drag and drop or click. Any common format works. You can also paste a direct .mp4 link.

2. Wait for transcript

The transcript (or AI summary) appears automatically — no button to click. For summary type, this takes a few extra seconds.

3. Edit gaps

  • Click any word to make it a gap (turns yellow).
  • Click a yellow word to remove the gap.
  • "✏️ Edit Text" lets you fix transcription mistakes.

4. Click "Make it happen"

AI fills in extra gaps based on the level you choose. Your manual selections are kept.

5. Share with students

  • Preview — See exactly what students will see.
  • Student Link — Copy and send. No signup needed.

How students use it (Transcript type)

  1. The video plays the first sentence automatically.
  2. They type the missing words.
  3. They can replay each sentence as many times as they want.
  4. Press Enter or click Next to advance.
  5. At the end, they see their score.

How students use it (Summary type)

  1. The full video plays at the top.
  2. They fill in all gaps in the summary below the video.
  3. They click Check Answers and see their score.
